Cultural Exchange

Cultural exchange plays a vital role in fostering mutual understanding and global cooperation. We provide valuable opportunities for learning and growth through international exchange programs, offering participants the chance to experience and appreciate different cultures firsthand. Our future plans include expanding these efforts with projects and cultural exchanges in Mexico, Guatemala, Peru, and Taiwan, further enriching cross-cultural connections and learning.

2021-2022 Guatemala Cultural Exchange

Coordinated volunteer initiatives in Guatemala, including cultural exchange activities at an orphanage and hands-on work at an organic farm, promoting sustainability, service, and cross-cultural understanding.

2024 US and Taiwan High School Exchange

Co-hosted a cultural exchange camp for 30 students from Taiwan's HsinChu and Chu-Pei Senior High Schools and U.S. schools, fostering cross-cultural friendships despite typhoon delays. The camp concluded with a final sharing session, highlighting strong connections between the students.

2024 US and Taiwan Online Cultural Exchange

Launched an online bilingual cultural exchange program in Fall 2024 for U.S. and Taiwan high school students, promoting mutual understanding through discussions in both languages on diverse topics.
